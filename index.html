About Me

I'm a 20-year-old college student currently pursuing a diploma in IT Web Programming at Nova Scotia Community College, where I'm diving into the world of modern web development. My coursework covers everything from networking and databases to web design, giving me a solid foundation in both the technical and creative sides of the web.

By day, I wrangle HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. By night... I still do that. I'm the kind of person who thinks semicolons are optional but emotional breakdowns over broken code are mandatory. I specialize in making things that look good and (usually) don’t crash. I love clean code, cool UI's, and snacks. Mostly snacks.

When I'm not coding, you can find me gaming, learning languages, reading, or attempting to cook something that doesn't involve a microwave. I also enjoy exploring new technologies and working on personal projects that challenge my skills. Recently, I've been experimenting with performance and accessibility best practices to make the web a better place for everyone.

Feel free to check out my projects below or reach out if you want to collaborate or just chat about the latest tech trends (or memes).
